Crown Ether Complexes 冠醚化合物

This project intends to develop a series of crown ether ligands for europium(II) luminescent complexes.
Including:

  • aza-15-crown-5
  • aza-12-crown-4
  • aza-18-crown-6
  • 1,13-diaza-18-crown-6
And other possible crown ether ligands.
